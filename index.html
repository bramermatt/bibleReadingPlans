One Year Chronological Bible Reading Plan

How It Works:

A Chronological Bible reading plan arranges Scripture in the order events occurred historically rather than the traditional book order. This approach helps readers follow the biblical narrative as it unfolds in time, providing better context and continuity.

  • 3-4 chapters per day (takes ~15–20 minutes daily)
  • Read the Bible in chronological order
  • Finish in 365 days (1 year)
